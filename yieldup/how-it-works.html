How it work

YieldUp is managing whole investment process starting from entering to transaction, selection and evaluation of projects, administration of payments and full cycle of debt collection process.

Nominal account

The transaction and payment accounting system on the platform is integrated with the accounting system of the licenced payment service provider who holds the nominal account. This allows automatically collect the funds for lending, distribute the received payments among investors, and generate the necessary financial reports. Nominal account is fully segregated account for customer security that hold the funds separated from company’s daily activities.
