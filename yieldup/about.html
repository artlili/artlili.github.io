About the platform

What is Yieldup for

YieldUp is European crowdlending platform created by experienced industry professionals and agreed with Latvia regulator FCMC. Company strategy based on transparency, security and integrity in all business areas and we proud our experience and care reputation. Our target is to give best investment experience to young professionals and entrepreneurs to make profit and raise funding for business needs in a modern way.

Growth
We help each SME in Europe to get better financing, supporting entrepreneurs’ to reach their dreams and business goals.
People
We unite thousands of people to collaborate for better economy in Europe. Anyone has a chance to contribute growth and earn stable monthly income.
Focus
New technologies, data management, trust and predictability, understanding of customer needs and targeted offerings are our focuses.
